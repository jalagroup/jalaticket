Privacy Policy

Jala Ticketing  ·  Jala Group  ·  Last updated: May 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Jala Group ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Jala Ticketing mobile application ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information – full name, email address, and phone number provided during registration.
  • Profile image – an optional photo you choose to upload.
  • Ticket data – details you enter when creating or responding to support tickets, including descriptions, attachments, and status updates.
  • Device token – a push notification token (FCM) used solely to deliver in-app notifications to your device.
  • Usage data – basic app interaction data used to improve performance and reliability.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To process and track support tickets.
  • To send push notifications about ticket updates and messages relevant to you.
  • To allow administrators to manage users within their organization.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider with row-level security. Data is stored in servers located in the European Union (Frankfurt, Germany). We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Push Notifications

The App uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send push notifications. You can disable notifications at any time in your device's system settings. Disabling notifications does not affect your ability to use the App.

5. Data Retention

Your account and ticket data are retained for as long as your account is active or as required to provide the service. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email below.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the App or by email.
